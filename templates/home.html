Provide Your Contaminated Feed

The Feed Sanitizer takes any news feed and turns it into straight RSS 2.0/ ATOM 1.0. This comes in handy if you are confronting with knocked up feeds that are barely well-formed and want to parse these with a not-so-solid piece of software. The Feed Sanitizer scrubs off the dirt and returns germ-free feeds.
